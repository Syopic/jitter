To reach our offices

Public Transport

Our offices can be reached by public transportation, the nearest tram stop being Blandonnet. For more information, visit the Transport Public Genevois website.

Train

Genève Cornavin is the main train station for the city of Geneva, and is located in downtown Geneva, about 25 minutes away from our offices. You can also choose to arrive at the Genève-Aéroport train station.

Geneva International Airport

Geneva International Airport is the closest airport. The Global Fund offices are located approximately 10 minutes by car from the airport.
